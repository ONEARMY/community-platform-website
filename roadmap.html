Where we are currently at:

Our development speed depends on the help we get

1. Run one community

Phase #1 - ✅ Completed

Currently we use the community Platform on Precious Plastic. A community that is all about recycling plastic. We live test and build out our features. At the moment it’s a community of over 300 workspaces and +5000 members signed up.

Visit our Precious Plastic community

2. Next setup two others

Phase #2 - Under development

Next we will deploy the platform to our other communities. One is building a community around fixing fashion, the other one around sustainable living. Both communities would use different features. This will deliver robust test results which we need for the next step.

3. Then we make it easy to DIY

Phase #3 - Near future

After deeply testing our own communities and being sure we developed the best features, we will design the platform to be easy to replicate. This step will require us to redesign our database structure and adding an admin panel. Making it easy for other community managers to setup and deploy.
